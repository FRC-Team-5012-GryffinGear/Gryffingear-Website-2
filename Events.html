Blood Drive ▼

Cedar Sinai will be running the blood drive in the school gym. Signing up ahead appointments are being accepted and preferred for the blood drive. There is a list of the Donors Guidelines, for your review, on the back side of our flyer. If you would like to donate blood please email Nardy Lopez at nardylopez70@gmail.com (or use the contact form on the website) with the time of day that you would prefer to set your appointment. You will get a voucher for a pint of ice cream for your donation of a pint of blood.

Summer Robotics Workshop ▼

The workshop is meant to help elementary and middle school students get involved with STEM though robotics. FIRST Robotics Team 5012, Gryffingear, and several other teams from the Antelope Valley will mentor students for six days. Students will collaborate to build a robot for a competition at the end of the week. Additional activities and speakers are scheduled to enhance the STEM experience. We are accepting 60 kids to attend with any other sign-ups being put on a wait list. Before the workshop, you will recieve a confirmation email about your child attending the workshop. It is a free event and lunch will not be provided. Please make sure your child brings something to eat each day. The workshop is starting July 14, 2014 - July 19, 2014 from 8:00am to 4:00pm, for more information feel free to email us.
